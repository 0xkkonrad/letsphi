Hey Phiers,

We hope you’re all having a fantastic week. We’re busy preparing and finalising our online events for the Autumn months. Watch this space for info and updates on future speakers.

In the meantime, we wanted to share some valuable resources that we think you might be interested in.

> AI ETHICS:

> - [ForHumanity University](https://www.linkedin.com/showcase/forhumanity-university/) is launching their course on Algorithm Ethics this Monday, September 19th 2022. This upcoming online course is completely free and comprises of 8 weeks, each with a different topics explored. You can register to the Algorithm Ethics Course [here](https://forhumanity.center/forhumanity-university/). You can also contact ForHumanity’s Executive Director [Ryan Carrier](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ryan-carrier-fhca-b286924/) if you wish to get involved with and know more about ForHumanity.

> COACHING AND MENTORSHIP:

> - If you’re interested in finding a mentor or a coach, or if you’d like to talk about ethics, philosophy and career/education goals, the [Global Catastrophic Risk Institute (GCRI)](https://gcrinstitute.org/) has an Advising and Collaboration Program. The Program is open now and will close sometime in October, so there are still a few weeks left to participate and get involved. Participation in the Program does not necessarily entail any significant time commitment. It can consist of anything from a short email exchange to more extensive project work. If you’re interested in speaking with GCRI or collaborating with them, you can find more info [here](https://gcrinstitute.org/open-call-for-advisees-and-collaborators-may-2022/).

> STARTING OUT IN AI ETHICS (shared by Ravit Dotan, PhD — speaker at our "From Philosophy to AI Ethics" workshop):

> - [FAQs](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/ravit-dotan_ai-aiethics-responsibleai-activity-6943227368761171968-gmPm/) when starting a career in AI ethics

> - [How to](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/ravit-dotan_ai-aiethics-activity-6913141979593736192-9g2w/) keep track of news in the AI ethics space

> - [How to](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/ravit-dotan_why-neural-networks-arent-neural-networks-activity-6888288682697072641-6D36/) get started learning about AI ethics

> - [Newsletter](https://andgovcheese.substack.com/) highlighting USA jobs for humanities and social science PhDs

This is everything from us, for now. We hope you’ll find these resources useful and that they’re going to help you with your education and career needs.
