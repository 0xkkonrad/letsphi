Hi Phiers,

We hope you’re all having a wonderful week. We would like to thank you for your continued support without which our work would not be possible! We also need your feedback to keep growing and providing you with even more career-related services and opportunities.

> We are adding a 'Testimonials' section to our [website](https://www.letsphi.com/) where we share the positive impact Let's Phi has had on philosophy students around the world.

> If our networking workshops and career events helped you in any manner, considering filling up this short [form](https://forms.gle/VCRkgMEJpPdbPmx99).

> Maybe Let’s Phi has helped you network with succesful professionals in your desired industry, or helped you determine wich industry appeals to you as a philosophy graduate. We might’ve even helped you find an internship, work opportunity or a trusted mentor. Whichever way you’ve benefitted from Let’s Phi, we would love to hear your experience and testimonial.

Your feedback will be selected and featured on the [Testimonials](https://www.letsphi.com/testimonials) section of our website. You have the option to be named alongside your testimonial or to remain anonymous.

We look forward to hearing from you. As always, don’t hesitate to reach out as we welcome all ideas and suggestions from our brilliant community members.

Best wishes,

The Let’s Phi Team.
