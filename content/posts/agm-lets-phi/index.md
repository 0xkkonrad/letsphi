Hey Phiers,

We're excited to invite you to our Annual General Meeting coming up soon.

**Let's Phi helps philosophers find career opportunities outside of academia. We run virtual conferences, workshops and networking events.** **We are a global community of 1800 members, 13 mentors and 4 team members.**

Even if you have never been involved in Let’s Phi, you can join the new exec team. Here are some reasons why you might want to join us:

- **Networking Opportunities:** Connect with professionals who have studied philosophy and have built successful careers outside of academia.
- **Career Boost:** Enhance your own career prospects by gaining valuable experiences, such as marketing, content creation, branding, growth, event organisation etc.
- **Content Creation**: Make a mark on the internet with your content.
- **Community Impact:** Contribute to a cause that helps fellow philosophers find their path in the non-academic world.
