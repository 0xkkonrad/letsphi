> Our speakers are [Steven Robinson](https://www.linkedin.com/in/steven-robinson-71163365/) and [Guanlan Mao](https://www.linkedin.com/in/guanlan-mao-a09851235/). The duo studied at Oxford University. Steven read philosophy and specialised in epistemology and metaphysics. Guanlan studied PPE (Philosophy, Politics and Economicis) and later moved to Clinical Psychology. Now they're leading [ARKN Ventures](https://www.linkedin.com/company/arkn-ventures/), a blockchain-centric research-focused Venture Capital firm. We meet them to talk how they made the switch from philosophy to VC and how they’re using their philosophical skills in this exciting field.
