Hi there,

We hope you enjoyed our last career event on Ethics Consulting. We were very happy to have Dr Kevin Macnish as our speaker. It’s now time to announce another exciting online workshop!

We have a career workshop on Web3 coming up in November that we think you will enjoy.

- **Saturday, November 12th 2022**
- **12 pm London time/ 1 pm CET (Central European Time)**
- **Online on Wonder Meetings**

> Our speaker for this workshop - Fotis Tsiroukis - is a Let’s Phi Community Member. Being between worlds, Fotis has one foot in academia and another in web3. Fotis went on a journey from Philosophy of Science to Cognitive Science and now back to the roots, doing a PhD in Philosophy of Open Science at the University of Exeter. Meanwhile, he has been actively involved in the world of crypto, decentralized organizations and internet activism exploring unorthodox means of work and value generation. He has been involved with MetaGame, Smart Contract Research Forum. Fotis is currently a KERNEL fellow and supporting projects in the space of crypto privacy and Decentralized Science.
