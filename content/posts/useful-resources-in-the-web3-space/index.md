Hi Phiers,

We hope you’re all having a wonderful week. Last Friday, we shared a round-up of useful resources on AI Ethics and Coaching/Mentoring. That post included info on the ForHumanity University’s free online course on Algorithm Ethics (it started this week), and the GCRI’s Advising and Collaboration Program. If you missed that post, you can read it [here](https://letsphi.substack.com/p/a-round-up-of-useful-resources-mentorship).

Today, we’d like to share some resources on Web3 that were kindly sent to us by Federico Ast, co-founder and CEO of Kleros. You might remember Federico from our July 7th event “The future of Law-Tech”. His company ‘Kleros’ is a decentralized justice system which uses blockchain and game theory for dispute resolution.

> Kleros is now launching a free online course on how to build in Web3. Federico gave us a sort of sneak-peak of the course by making these two videos available to our community members:

> [VIDEO 1](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kAcZSGIrF-4): How to build in Web3: The Blockchain Revolution

> [VIDEO 2](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JTKB5DjyOOY): How to build in Web3: Strategy Design

Follow [Kleros](https://www.linkedin.com/company/kleros/) for updates on when this free, online course launches. You can also have a look at their [website](https://kleros.io/) to see what they offer and how they operate. If you’re interested in Web3 and want to start conversations on it, you can connect with Federico on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/federicoast/).

This is everything from us, for now. We hope you’ll find these resources useful and that they’re going to help you with your education and career needs.
