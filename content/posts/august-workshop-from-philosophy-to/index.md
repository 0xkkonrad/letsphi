Hey there,

We have a new, exciting career workshop coming up at the end of this month that we think you’re going to enjoy.

- **August 20th 2022**
- **11 am BST/ 12 pm CET**
- **Online on Wonder**

> [Joshua Bucheli](https://www.linkedin.com/in/joshua-bucheli/) works for Cyberunity AG, a recruitment company specialised in cybersecurity. He is an expert in data protection, digital forensics, incident response and IT risk, governance and compliance. Before landing a position in cybersecurity recruitment, Joshua was a Contributor, Fellow and Chairman of the Ethics Committee at ForHumanity, as well as Head of Let’s Phi Community Management. He has a BA in Politics and Philosophy from Durham University and an MA in Political, Legal and Economic Philosophy from the University of Bern. He’s going to talk about his career progression from philosophy to cybersecurity.
