Hi there,

There’s still time to register for our upcoming career workshop on building a career in content marketing.

“From Philosophy To Content Marketing”, is *this Thursday*. Here are the details:

- Thursday, June 15th 2023
- 5 pm London Time/6 pm CET (Central European Time)
- Online on Microsoft Teams

> Our speaker, Alex Yates (PhD), is going to tell us all about how he built his career in content marketing with a philosophy background. Alex is originally from the state of Maine in the U.S. After finishing his undergraduate studies at a small liberal arts college, he went to the University of St Andrews in Scotland, completing an MLitt and staying on to write a doctoral dissertation on Frege's philosophy of logic. Academic work proved elusive, but after working a few jobs back in the U.S., he was hired as a content marketer, first as a freelancer and then as a full-time employee. The abilities to learn fast, write clearly, and see the big picture, which Alex honed in philosophy studies, has proven invaluable for a professional business writing career and he is going to tell us how to harness them to our advantage in the workforce.
