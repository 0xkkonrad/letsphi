Let's Phi helps philosophers find career opportunities outside of academia. We run virtual conferences, workshops and networking events, and we share the stories of philosophers who built fulfilling careers beyond the university.

We are a global community of **1,800+ members**, **13 mentors** and a small volunteer team.

## Why Let's Phi?

Philosophy attracts exceptionally intelligent people. It gives them great analytical skills, open-mindedness and a lot of stamina for going deep on a topic. Philosophy departments all over the world are great at preparing you for an academic career — but outside of academia, it's not obvious what a philosophy graduate can do.

Let's Phi exists to change that. We help you leverage your philosophy skills for a non-academic career: find out how to market your degree, and get inspired by people who have already made the leap.
