## St Andrews

The School of Philosophy at the University of St Andrews is a top-ranked school in the [UK](https://www.theguardian.com/education/ng-interactive/2022/sep/24/the-guardian-university-guide-2023-the-rankings) and [globally](https://www.topuniversities.com/university-subject-rankings/philosophy). However, rankings alone do not guarantee a job — especially with a humanities degree.

### What we do

Let's Phi St Andrews exists to alleviate students' anxiety over employability and prove that philosophy graduates build fulfilling careers *thanks to*, rather than despite, their degree.

Philosophy students are equipped for a wide range of careers — but they need to see it to believe it! Real stories of philosophy graduates' journeys are the most empowering thing a student can hear.

Thanks to Let's Phi's network, the St Andrews chapter organises offline and online talks, workshops and networking sessions to connect current and former philosophy students.

### Getting involved

- **You have a philosophy degree and work outside academia?** You could inspire young people to consider your field. We organise both in-person and online events where students can explore what's right for them, network, and learn what it takes to succeed.
- **You're a current St Andrews student?** Join the team and help with outreach, logistics or marketing.
